Chili con Carne

Description

A typical mexican dish, full of flavor and simple to do.

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground Beef
  • 1 can Black Beans
  • 1 can Red Beans
  • 1/2 can Sweet Corn
  • 2 cans Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • Seasoning..

Steps

  1. Stir fry the Onion and the Peppers
  2. Add the beef and the seasoning, let it cook until the meat is brownish
  3. Add the Tomato Sauce
  4. Add the Beans
  5. Add the Sweet Corn
  6. Let is simmer 30 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
